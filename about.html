About Me




Welcome to my portfolio !

Hi there! I'm Ritik Mishra, a passionate game designer and 3D artist. I love creating immersive experiences through captivating gameplay and visually stunning worlds.

With a background in game design and a strong artistic flair, I bring a unique blend of creativity and technical expertise to my projects. I have a deep understanding of game mechanics, level design principles, and storytelling, which allows me to craft engaging gameplay experiences that resonate with players.

As a 3D artist, I specialize in creating high-quality assets and environments. I have a keen eye for detail and a solid grasp of modeling, texturing, and lighting techniques. I enjoy pushing the boundaries of visual fidelity and strive to create breathtaking visuals that enhance the overall gaming experience.

Throughout my career, I have worked on various projects, ranging from indie games to AAA titles. I thrive in collaborative environments and enjoy working closely with cross-functional teams to bring ideas to life. I am proficient in industry-standard software such as Unity, Blender and Substance painter.

I am constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to expand my skills and knowledge in the ever-evolving world of game design and 3D art. I believe in the power of games to entertain, educate, and inspire, and I am dedicated to creating memorable experiences that leave a lasting impact on players.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I invite you to explore my work and get in touch to discuss any potential collaboration or project.
